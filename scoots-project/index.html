Scoots Escape Rentals

See the Real Cozumel!

Scoots Escape Rental is for the adventurer that wants to get off the beaten path and see the real Cozumel! One of the most beautiful islands in the world, Cozumel is renowned for it’s beautiful coral reefs that attract divers from around the world and our ancient Mayan ruins that are on everyone’s bucket list to see in person.

We have two locations to better serve you - at the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and one at the Terminal Puerta Maya for the convenience of our cruise patrons. We take reservations and walk-ins are always welcome. Whether you're looking to travel the island on your own for a day or are with a large group, we can fit your needs. Our scooters can seat one or two people, while our jeeps can accomodate up to five adults. Rent one of our scooters, ATVs, or jeeps to drive yourself around the island and see the many amazing places Cozumel has to offer. We have the best prices on the island and hope to be your first choice for your rental needs!
