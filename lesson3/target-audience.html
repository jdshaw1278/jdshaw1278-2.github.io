Who

Education Required

Motivation

Site is for those wishing to check on best gear for comfort of students that will be exposed to weather elements.

Scenarios

Is the site easily accessibly?

Yes, the site is designed to be read easily on a variety of media formats and with minimal bandwidth.

Will the site show me other locations?

Currently, we only cover a few towns in Southeast Idaho. But we plan to expand our venture and hope to be a valuable tool to students and parents nationwide.

What will the site show me?

We will have current weather conditions with recommendations for proper attire for students that will be outside. During inclement weather, we will regularly update school closures and delays.

Is the site free?

Yes, we intend the site to be free for use, but will have minor ads.
